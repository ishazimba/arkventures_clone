About Us

Ark Ventures

Since its inception in 2012, has been working actively in the field of Marketing, Research & Training. ARK Ventures following on its tagline of “Grow with US” has always put “Client First” at the forefront of whatever we do. This can be easily reflected on the list of the clients that we have been able to maintain since our inception in 2012. From leading global brands like Google, Mahindra, Lava to prominent national brands like CG, NCell, FNCCI, Surya Nepal are few of our many “Happy Clients”. Our partnership with City & Guilds a Royal Charter of UK and Manipal Global Education Services further endorses our strength.

At ARK Ventures we strive to provide best of the services to our clients. Get involved with us for all your Marketing, Research, Training and Project Implementation requirements. We have a large pool of in-house and contractual both national and international human resources and subject matter experts who are forerunners in their respective domains. Why wait, Get Involve with Us now.

ARK Ventures after its inception in 2012, was mostly involved in providing Marketing & Research services to Domestic and International organizations till 2015. Since 2015 ARK Ventures has evolved more into an investment company with its investment portfolio focused mostly in the Training sector. In 2016 ARK Ventures was successful to join hands with City & Guilds-UK and Manipal Global Education Services-India to run various vocational, technical and professional education qualifications in Nepal.

Services

MARKETING & RESEARCH

Companies today are spending tons of money on advertising to ensure that their products and services reach the masses. However, we believe that through right tools and research, companies can ensure more profit with less spending. Our objective is to break the conventional way of doing marketing. We specialize in market research, interfacing with customers and campaign implementations. With innovative ways we aim to represent our clients in both residential and commercial markets effectively. We are committed to fulfilling our clients’ customer, business and objective acquisition goals by operating quality programs.

TRAININGS

Corporate & Retail : One of the main components of ARK Ventures is its Training wing. ARK Ventures has capacity to provide various corporate trainings pertaining to Human Resources Development, Information Technology, Language, Occupational Health/Safety Training, Trainings on Sales & Marketing, etc. ARK Ventures has been awarded the affiliation rights to run Diploma and Advance Diploma Level training under City & Guilds-UK and Manipal Global Education Services-India hence becoming the first authorized center for the same in Nepal. ARK Ventures is now running training classes in the sectors like Construction, Hospitality, IT and Corporate Trainings.
